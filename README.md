## English

### What is this?

**skotm's RNG** is a browser-based aura-collecting RNG game.  
Roll the dice, discover auras, and work your way from *1-in-2* **Common** all the way up to *1-in-100-trillion* **The Beginning**.

Over **~170 auras** span 12 rarity tiers. Earn Aether, upgrade your stats, and compete on the global leaderboard.

---

This name appears on the global ranking board and must be unique.

Sources of Aether:

- **Per roll** — 1 base (+ 10 on a Lucky Roll)

- **Duplicates** — each extra copy converts (capped at 50 per aura)
- **Achievements** — one-time rewards on unlock

### Tips

> **Prioritise Luck first.** Early Luck upgrades give the biggest relative gain — a ×5 multiplier turns a 1-in-1,000,000 aura into effectively 1-in-200,000.

> **Save Lucky Rolls for rare hunts.** Don't let a charged Lucky Roll fire off during casual grinding — toggle Fast Roll off when you're close to a charge to time it intentionally.

> **Stack boosts before a big die.** Activate an Aether Flask and Prism Core *before* using a Mega / Hyper Lucky Die to maximise both Aether gains and Mutation odds at once.

> **Set the cutscene threshold.** In Settings → *Skip confirm cutscene*, set a value so the reveal animation only plays for auras above a rarity you care about. Auto-rolling stays fast.

### 攻略ヒント

> **まずLuckを優先。** 序盤のLuckアップグレードが最大のコスパ。×5倍なら1/100万が実質1/20万に化けます。

> **Lucky Rollはレア狙い専用に。** チャージしたLucky Rollを無駄に使わないよう、Legendary以上のレアを狙うときに意図的に発動しましょう。Fast Rollをオフにしてチャージタイミングをコントロールするのがコツです。

> **ハイパーダイス前にブーストを重ねる。** Mega/Hyper Lucky Dieを使う前にAether FlaskとPrism Coreを同時に発動して、Aether獲得とMutation確率を同時に最大化しましょう。

> **カットシーン閾値を設定。** 設定の「Skip confirm cutscene」で「1 in X 以上のときだけカットシーンを再生」に設定できます。Auto-rollingでのCommon/Epicの遅延をゼロにできます。
